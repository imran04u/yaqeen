From our ancestor’s time, they did various business. They started the business by selling cows. They say their cow’s gives morning 5 litre milk and evening 4 litre milk. So they don’t reduce cow’s prices in their deals.

They get the buyer farmer address and send their favourite cow to their home. After 2 or 3 days or after a week they go to buyers house and check Whether cow gives milk as told by them or not. They will check, are the cow buyers happy? If buyers liked the cow, they give one time food and pay the amount. That much they were special in their business.

They did business without thinking of more profit or becoming rich. Their only aim was service to people. If cow did not give milk as they said they bring back to their home. After sales, they provide all the needs of their assisting workers and families make them happy. Then they took the balance amount to home.

Also they manufactured their needs for their home, for example: dhal, oil etc. Seeing this, their neighbour too asked some for sample use. In less cost they gave quality products.
